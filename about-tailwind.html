My Inspiration ♡

Hello! My name is Allison, and this is my project, Tobidoki. I love adorable and cozy designs, which inspired me to create a soft, game-like website experience.

This project was inspired by virtual pet games and cute digital experiences, often seen in Asian-style pet and simulation games, but reimagined into a simple and relaxing web format.

About Tobidoki ♡

Tobidoki is a cozy interactive website where users can choose a character and explore themed spaces.

It focuses on soft visuals, simple interactions, and a calm atmosphere, creating a gentle experience similar to a digital pet.
